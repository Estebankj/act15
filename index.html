Bachelor in Applied Computing Technology

The BACT is a four year degree program that focuses on the practical aspects of the Science of Computing. The developed core skills in multimedia technology web development and network/system administration. Furthermore, it prepairs learners with supervisory and management skills. The curriculum is drive from be BS Industrial technology major in computer technology with CHED Certificat of Program Compliance(COCP) No.13 scries of 2021.


PROGRAM OBJECTIVES:

The program prepare learners with the practical skills in multimedia technoplogy, web mobile development and network systems administration supervisional skills to manage an organization in this areas of competencies engage in a startup business of purpose a teaching career.

SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES

The learners should be able to:

  1. Demonstrate understanding of the importance of being an ethical computing professional when making work-related.
  2. Devalop products or processes by applying their knowledge in computer network system, and development of productivity tools.
  3. Participate as an active member of a development service team with leadership roles when appropriate.
  4. Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals, processes or products.
  5. Make possitiuve contribution to the community with the skills and abilities learned in the program.
  6. pursue lifelong learning for personal and professional growth through valid experimental and field-basess opportunities.
